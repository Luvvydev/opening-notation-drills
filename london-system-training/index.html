London System

London System training that tests the moves, not just the idea

The London is popular because the setup is clear, but players still forget move order details and common plans. Drilling the positions helps the setup become automatic.

Train the London System Try the demo
Built for recall

You play the move, get feedback, and repeat the line until the pattern is easier to find in a real game.

What to practice in the London

Focus on the recurring setup, the bishop development, central control, and when the position calls for normal development or a sharper reaction.

Why drill it

The London can feel simple enough to wing. That is where bad habits start. Repetition helps separate the real setup from lazy autopilot moves.
