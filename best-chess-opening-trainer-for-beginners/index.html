What beginners need

A beginner opening trainer should be simple: one position, one move, immediate feedback, and enough repetition to make the pattern stick. Long theory files are easy to save and hard to use.

Good first openings

The London System, Italian Game, Ruy Lopez, Caro-Kann Defense, and Sicilian Defense are common starting points because they lead to recurring structures and plans.

How ChessDrills fits

ChessDrills turns opening lines into playable drills. You do not just look at a move order. You have to make the move on the board.
