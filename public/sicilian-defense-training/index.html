Sicilian Defense training for remembering the first moves under pressure

The Sicilian creates sharp, uneven positions early. That is useful only if you can remember the move order and the reason behind the setup.

What to practice in the Sicilian

Start with the first move orders, central pawn structure, development plan, and the moments where Black must respond accurately instead of playing generic development.

Why drill it

The Sicilian has many branches. A drill based approach keeps the first layer manageable before adding more variations.
